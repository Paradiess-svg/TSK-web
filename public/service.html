What is

Odoo is a leading edge enterprise resource planning (ERP) and customer relationship management (CRM) system based on open source technologies with a worldwide presence.

The solution was developed by Odoo S.A. a Belgium based company with a global R&D presence. This ERP system can be specifically tailored and customized for the requirement of any industry, it not only addresses the current requirements of your company but it also provides you with the opportunity of continually improve and refine your business processes.

You can pick out a module for your current requirement and grow with it by adding other modules as you deem fit.

It is used by more than 2 million users worldwide to manage companies of all different sizes for; Mining | Real Estate | Hospital | Education | Logistics | Manufactures

Customer Relationship Management

An integrated information system that is used to manage plan, schedule, and control post-sales and pre-sales activities in an organization.

1. Leads

The lead module organizes all of the clients data here. This CRM module has smart views for assessing the current state of leads.

2. Marketing

The marketing module organizes all of your email marketing campaigns, as well as other information.

3. Workflows

The workflow module helps you to automate your sales process. CRM's workflow module allows you to collect all of your processes in one place and examine the number of triggers, among other things.

4. Reports

Reports module of the CRM software gathers reports of different categories and presents them in one place.

5. Content Repository

The content repository module of the CRM software gathers all images and documents. It makes them easily accessible whenever you need them.

Human Resource Information System

An HRIS helps you recruit, hire, and train new employees as quickly as possible so that they can effectively fill their new roles. With better initial onboarding and continual training, you can boost employee productivity, reduce turnover, and achieve greater efficiency.

1. Attendance Management

Track the attendance of each employee through the HR software system.

2. Employee Contract Management

Manage employee contracts in real-time on the go and get automatic notifications when the contract period is about to expire.

3. Leaves Management

Simplify the process of applying for employee leave, automate the update of leave allowances, and easily manage other conditions.

4. Expenses Management

Manage cash advances, allowances, reimbursements, and more easily and generate reports accurately.

5. Payroll Management

The payroll application calculates salary accurately based on working time, overtime hours, and employee attendance.
