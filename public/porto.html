It Start From Here

PT. Tetamba Studio Kreasi was established in February 2015 in the city of Depok. Gathering final year students from the Faculty of Computer Science, University of Indonesia to conduct research and development on web, android, and iOS in accordance with industry standards.

Based on Deed of Establishment No. 224 dated 20 February 2019 made before Hj. Netty Maria Machdar, SH, as ratified by Decree of the Minister of Law and Human Rights of the Republic of Indonesia No. AHU- 0010419.AH.01.01. In 2019 February 26 2019, domiciled at Griya Rahmani 2 A/5 Complex. JI. H. Kodja 2, Kel. Steamed Kec. Beji, Depok City, Prop. West Java hereinafter referred to as "Tetamba".

We are a professional IT consulting company that provides solutions to every technology problems for your company. Our team has assisted our various business partners in overcoming technology challenges and increasing their business productivity.

We start our first web and mobile application project for Owlexa Healthcare, a company by Lintasarta. Currently, we have worked on mobile and web application for several companies. Such as Bank Mandiri, BRI, Jamkrindo, etc.

After 8 years of dedication we already make a big number of project from 10+ client including 3 big BUMN company, and mastering 15+ programming language & framework. With our proffesional and dedicated worker, anything is possible .See more in our product, our team and in our client.
